Winter's Wealth

Winter is here, so get warm and cosy with Winter’s Wealth! Match numbers and win a prize. If you reveal a bonus prize, you win it instantly! It’s a winter wonderland of winnings!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Press each Pine Cone symbol in the LUCKY NUMBERS panel to reveal a number.

Press each Snowflake symbol in the YOUR NUMBERS panel to reveal a number.

Match any LUCKY NUMBER to any identical YOUR NUMBER to win the corresponding prize.

Press the BONUS button for a chance to reveal an instant prize value.

When all the LUCKY NUMBERS, YOUR NUMBERS and the BONUS tile are revealed, the game ends. A closing message then appears indicating the game’s outcome.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
